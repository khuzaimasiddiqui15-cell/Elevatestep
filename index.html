FAQ

Common Questions

Will people be able to tell I'm wearing them?+
No. The insoles sit entirely inside your shoe — completely hidden. From outside your footwear looks totally normal.
What shoe types work best?+
High-top sneakers, boots, and shoes with depth work best. Low-top sneakers work fine with 3cm. For 5cm+ we recommend mid-ankle or high-top shoes.
How much height do I actually get?+
1 layer = 3cm, 2 layers = 5cm, 3 layers = 7cm, 4 layers = 9cm. Most buyers note it can add slightly more due to the air cushion spring effect.
Do I need a bigger shoe size?+
For 3–5cm your current shoes should be fine. For 7–9cm, consider shoes half to one size larger, especially if they fit snugly.
How do I get the right fit?+
Each insole has printed trim lines on the bottom. Select the size closest to yours and trim with scissors. Takes about 30 seconds.
What's your return policy?+
30 days, no questions asked. If you're not happy for any reason, reach out and we'll refund you in full.
